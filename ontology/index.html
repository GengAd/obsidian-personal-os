üìÅ Operational‚Äã

‚ÄúHow?‚Äù ‚Äì entities that structure, repeat, or execute goal-directed behavior

Strategic‚Äã

Orchestrating + Occurring ‚Äì entities that structure behavior toward a defined end

  • Project ‚Äì a structured initiative coordinating tasks to achieve a goal
  • Mission
  • Plan ‚Äì a roadmap that defines steps toward a future outcome
  • Course ‚Äì a structured program of sequential learning activities with a defined end
  • Process ‚Äì a structured sequence of operations designed for repeatable results with known boundaries

Iterative‚Äã

Orchestrating + Recurring ‚Äì entities that coordinate ongoing cycles of structured behavior

  • Responsibility ‚Äì an ongoing obligation to uphold standards or manage a domain
  • Cycle ‚Äì a repeating phase-based operational structure, often tied to strategic goals

Tactical‚Äã

Executing + Occurring ‚Äì entities that represent direct, focused units of work

  • Task ‚Äì a discrete, actionable unit of execution
  • Combat ‚Äì a direct, attention-intensive problem or confrontation
  • Meeting ‚Äì a coordinated session for synchronization or decision-making
  • Training ‚Äì a focused activity aimed at developing or refining skill

Repetitive‚Äã

Executing + Recurring ‚Äì entities that involve regular, direct-action loops

  • Routine ‚Äì a sequence of actions regularly repeated to maintain standards
  • Exercise ‚Äì a structured action performed for development or reinforcement
  • Drill ‚Äì a high-precision repetition used for mastery or emergency response

Entities that represent personal or subjective insight derived from observation or journaling
